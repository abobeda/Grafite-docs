The **References** screen is the fastest way to see your entire visual library at once.

Instead of opening boards one by one, Grafite gathers every saved image into a single immersive gallery. This gives you a wider view of your references, patterns, colors, subjects, and recurring ideas.

## The Reference Wall

The References screen uses a spacious **Reference Wall** layout.

Images fill the screen in a visual grid so you can scan by feeling, shape, color, and composition, not only by file name. This is useful when you are looking for inspiration, rediscovering older material, or noticing connections between boards.

## What you can do here

1. Browse all references saved across your boards.
2. Search by image name or tag.
3. Filter references by board.
4. Explore references by color family.
5. Open any image in Focus View.
6. Sort references by newest, oldest, or title.

## When to use it

Use the References screen when you want to search your full library, compare images across multiple boards, or browse freely without starting from a specific moodboard.
