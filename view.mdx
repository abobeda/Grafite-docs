## The View button shows zoom and rotation controls.

- `Zoom`: type a zoom percent or use the preset buttons `10%`, `100%`, `250%`, and `1600%`.
- `Rotation`: type a rotation angle or use the preset buttons `0°`, `90°`, `180°`, and `270°`.
- The lock button next to zoom stops zoom changes.
- The lock button next to rotation stops rotation changes.

## You can also move the view without opening this menu.

- Mouse wheel: zoom in and out.
- `Shift` \+ mouse wheel: rotate the canvas.
- Hold `Space` and drag with the mouse: pan the canvas.
- One finger touch drag: pan the canvas.
- Two finger touch gesture: pan, zoom, and rotate together.
