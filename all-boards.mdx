The **All boards** section shows every moodboard in your Grafite library.

Each board is a visual collection. You can use boards to gather references for a project, art direction, character, environment, product idea, visual style, or anything else you want to organize.

<Frame>
  ![Boards 1](/images/boards-1.png)
</Frame>

## What you can do here

1. Browse all boards in your library.
2. Open a board to view and manage its references.
3. See a preview of the images inside each board.
4. Quickly compare different visual directions.
5. Use **Organize boards** to change the board order.

## Opening a board

Click any board to open it.

Inside a board, you can add images, rename references, reorder items, remove references, set a cover image, and inspect references in Focus View.
