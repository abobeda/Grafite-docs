## The brush button opens the brush panel and also picks the Brush tool.

### Brushes in Grafite:

- `Monoline`

- `Ink Sketch`

- `Soft Pencil`

- `Charcoal Pencil`

## What the brush panel does:

- Lets you pick a brush.

- Shows a preview picture for each brush.

- Remembers size for each brush.
