Grafite is a focused, modern sketch and drafting app built for Windows. Designed for fast ideas, clean lines, and a natural pen-first workflow, Grafite gives you the freedom of drawing on paper with the control of digital tools. From expressive brushes and precise layers to rich color tools, artboards, and flexible export options, Grafite is made to help you move from first mark to finished work with clarity, speed, and confidence.

Grafite was proudly built by Alexandre Bobeda, a visual designer, artist, and creative coder.
