**Grafite** is a focused, modern moodboard and visual reference app built for Windows. It helps you collect images, organize them into boards, browse your visual archive, and keep creative references close while you work.

Instead of scattering inspiration across folders, downloads, screenshots, and browser tabs, Grafite gives you one calm place to gather everything. You can create boards from images, import folders as boards, review all saved references, search and filter your library, and open images in a focused viewer when you want to inspect details.

![Grafite Home](/images/grafite-home.png)

Grafite is made for artists, designers, illustrators, creative directors, game developers, and anyone who works visually. It is built to feel fast, polished, and quiet, so your references stay organized without getting in the way of your thinking.

Grafite was proudly built by Alexandre Bobeda, a visual and product designer, artist, and creative coder.
