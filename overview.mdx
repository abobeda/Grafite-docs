Grafite is like a big piece of paper on your computer.

You can draw, erase, smudge, pick colors, move layers, bring in pictures, and export your work in different ways.

This help explains every part of the app in very simple words.

# Canvas

canvas image

1. The big middle area is the canvas. This is your drawing space.
2. The left side has your drawing tools.
3. The little buttons next to the left side help you change brush size and brush opacity.
4. The top left has the file menu, the Gallery button, and the name of your sketch.
5. The top right has view, import, export, and canvas controls.
6. Some side panels can pop out. You can drag them away from the edge and dock them back again.
