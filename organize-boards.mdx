## Sorting options

1. **Latest** shows recently updated boards first.
2. **A to Z** sorts boards alphabetically by title.

## How to use it

1. Click the **Organize boards** button in the top right.
2. Choose a sorting option.
3. Grafite updates the board order immediately.

Your selected sorting mode is saved, so Grafite can keep the same board order the next time you open the app.
