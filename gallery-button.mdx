## The four-squares button on top left opens the Gallery.

- The Gallery shows your recent sketches.
- Clicking a sketch card opens it.
- `Clear recent files` removes the recent items from the Gallery list.
- The big `+` button in the Gallery makes a new canvas.
- Grafite keeps recent sketches in the Gallery automatically when you switch sketches or go back to the Gallery.

## Sketch name box

- The name box is where you name your sketch.
- Press `Enter` to keep the new name.
- Press `Escape` while typing to go back to the old name.
- This name is also used when Grafite suggests an export file name.
