The **Recently added** section shows the newest reference images saved to your Grafite library.

It helps you quickly return to images you just imported, saved, or added to a board.

<Frame>
  ![Recently Added](/images/recently-added.jpg)
</Frame>

## What you can do here

1. Review your newest saved references.
2. Open a reference in Focus View.
3. Jump back into recent visual research.
4. Quickly inspect images after importing them.
5. Use it as a lightweight history of recent additions.

## When this is useful

Use **Recently added** after importing a group of images or folders. It gives you a fast way to check that your latest references were added correctly.
