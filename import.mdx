## The Import button has two choices.

- `Images`: imports a picture file.

- `Paste from clipboard`: imports an image that is already copied.

## Image file import currently supports:

- `.png`

- `.jpg`

- `.jpeg`

- `.webp`

## When you import an image:

- Grafite makes a new layer called `Imported`.

- The picture appears ready to place.

- Drag it to where you want it.

- Click outside the picture to lock it in place.

## After an image is already placed:

- Hold `Ctrl` and drag the image to move it with the mouse.

- On touch, press and hold on the image, then drag to move it.

- On touch, lift your finger when the image is where you want it.
