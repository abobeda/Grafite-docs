# Basic gestures

If you are using Grafite on a touch screen, this is the simple rule:

<Frame>
  ![Canvas Rhino Touch](/images/canvas-rhino-touch.jpg)
</Frame>

- Tap buttons to open menus and panels.
- Tap options inside panels to choose them.
- Touch and drag on the canvas with one finger to pan.
- Use two fingers on the canvas to pan, zoom, and rotate.

## How touch users open the little extra menus:

- `File`, `View`, `Import`, `Export`, and `Canvas`: tap the button once.
- `Brushes`, `Eraser`, `Layers`, and `Colors`: tap the button once.
- `Colors` extra options: tap the small `More options` button at the top-right of the Colors panel.
- `Canvas` presets: tap `Presets`.
- `Layer` actions: tap the three-dots button on a layer row, or long-press the layer row itself.

## Touch-only helpers:

- To move an already placed imported image, press and hold on the image, then drag it.
- To edit canvas width or height on touch, press and hold on the `W` or `H` field.
- To reorder layers on touch, press and hold a layer row, then drag it.

## Important touch note:

- Removing a draft palette swatch is right-click-only in the current build, so there is not a special touch remove gesture for that action yet.
