## The Export button lets you save your work in different ways.

Formats:

- `JPEG`: a smaller flat image. It always includes a background.
- `PNG`: a flat image. It can keep the background on or off.
- `PSD`: a Photoshop file with layers.
- `Grafite`: a native `.gft` file with editable layers, strokes, and raster content.

## Area choices:

- `Canvas screenshot`: saves what you are looking at right now.
- `Full drawing`: saves all visible drawing content.
- `Artboard`: saves just the artboard area.

<Tip>
  Good to know:

  - `Artboard` export only works when an artboard is turned on.
</Tip>

## Scale choices:

- `100%`
- `200%`
- `400%`

## Background toggle:

- For `PNG` and `PSD`, you can choose whether the paper/background is included.
- For `JPEG`, background is always on.
- For `Grafite`, the background toggle does not matter because the file keeps the full document.

<Info>
  Important:

  - Grafite does not have a separate `Save` button in this build. Files are saved automatically.
  - If you want an editable file, use `Export` and choose `Grafite`.
</Info>
